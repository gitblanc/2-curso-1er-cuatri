Welcome to our Internet Book Shop System! Here you can see the main window of the program.
To add a book to your order, just select it from the upper list and then press the button "Add" (Ctrl + D)
To remove it, select it from the bottom list and press "Remove" (Ctrl + R)
You can reset your order by pressing "Reset" (Ctrl + R)
To confirm your order press "Buy" and your receipt will be printed.
